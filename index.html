How to Order

1. Choose Your Food

Browse our diverse menu and select your favorite Indonesian dishes and beverages.

2. Add to Cart

Adjust quantities and add items to your cart. Click the cart icon to review your order.

3. Fill Details & Pay

Proceed to checkout, fill in your delivery details, and choose your preferred payment method.

4. Confirm via WhatsApp

Your order details will be sent to us via WhatsApp for quick confirmation and processing.

5. Enjoy Delivery

Relax and wait! Your freshly prepared meal will be delivered right to your doorstep.
